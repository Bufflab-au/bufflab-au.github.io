Who we are

About Buff Lab

A passion project turned proper — Buff Lab is built on years of hands-on detailing experience and a genuine love for paint.

We're not a big detailing shop trying to do everything. Buff Lab specialises in two things only — paint correction and ceramic coating application. That focus means every job gets the full attention it deserves.

Based in Brisbane and fully mobile, we come to you — at home, at work, or wherever suits. Flexible appointments available to fit around your schedule.

Every job is treated with care and precision. We take pride in the results we deliver — because your car deserves more than a rushed job.
